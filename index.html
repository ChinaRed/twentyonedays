Juice Recipe

Juice is best to be made every morning, in large batches

Juice the following together

  • Apples
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Beats

After done juicing, blend in the following with the juice

  • Lettuce
  • Kale
  • Blackberries

Daily Schedule

Diet

  • Juice (morning and throughout day)
  • Fruits (throughout day)
  • Steamed Veggies (daily dinner)
  • Steamed Fish or Chicken (every other dinner)

Supplements

Take these supplements first thing in the morning (empty stomach)

  • Psyllium Husk - 1 tbsp w/ mix w/ 12 oz water, let thicken about 1 min and drink (start daily on day 10, empty stomach)
  • Baking Soda - 1 tsp mix w/ 12oz water (daily, empty stomach)
  • Buffered Vitamin C - 1 heaping tbsp mix w/ 16oz water (every 4 days, empty stomach)
  • Castor & Turpentine - mix 1 tbsp Turpentine and 1 tsp Castor Oil, drink, follow with glass of water (every 7 days, empty stomach)
  • Probiotic (daily, empty stomach or between meals)

Activity

  • 1 hour of sun a day when possible
  • 5 mile hike/walk in nature daily when not raining
  • 15-20 minutes of breathing exercise
  • 20 minutes of stretching daily

Restrictions

Drugs

  • Stop all medications or drugs. Only use painkiller for headaches as the last resort. Painkillers will affect liver function which ultimately lead to digestive problems.

Food

  • No Dairy
  • No Yogurt
  • No Pasta
  • No Rice
  • No meat othe than steamed fish or chicken every other day

Liquids

  • Distilled water (32oz per day)
  • Only fresh juices you make
  • Organic Tea (using distilled water)
  • No coffee
  • No soda
  • No fruit juices (unless fresh juiced)

Sleep

  • No electronic usage 1 hour prior to sleep
  • Make sure room is completely dark at night (buy blackout curtains if necessary)
  • No phones or electronics at bedside
  • Try to go to sleep by 9pm every night (Not permanent but it optimizes circadian clock)
  • Nap as often as you feel like… the more the better if you are tired
  • Wear Breath EZ strips to sleep if they don't bother you
